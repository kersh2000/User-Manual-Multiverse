BRADLEY KERSHAW: User Manual

Styles Values Patience Communication Help Misunderstood Personality Images
    My Styles:
  • I work very well independently for long periods of time, once I get into the swing of things and fully understand what I am working on.
  • I like to break large problems down into smaller chunks, which allows me to easily create a clear, organised, and structured plan for a task.
  • I enjoy teaching people new concepts or ideas which they are either struggling to understand or want or learn more about.
    What I value:
  • People who are open-minded and not afraid to change or accept new ideas.
  • Those who are patient and understand that everyone is human, and we all make mistakes.
  • Not being afraid to give criticism. Receiving criticism is a great way for people to self-reflect and learn from their mistakes but giving criticism in a generous way is equally important in order to efficiently work in a team!
    What I don't have patience for:
  • A lack of acknowledgement and recognition! I highly value people who will have the patience to listen to everyone's ideas or fully respond to everyone's concerns before moving on or being ignored
  • People who avoid taking the blame / being accountable when they have made an error. A problem cannot often be solved unless the root cause is first identified, because everyone makes mistakes, which is a truth that we should not frown upon.
    How to best communicate with me:
  • Emails and Teams are a standard for me, but for a more informal chat or request, WhatsApp is preferred. Video calls and in-person meetings are also another viable option.
    How to help me:
  • Asking me if I need help; I often enjoy working independently and will try my best to solve an issue on my own before asking for assistance.
  • If there is a large problem that needs solving, which I do not understand, breaking the problem down into smaller steps is often the best way for me to fully understand the context of that problem, in order to begin problem-solving.
  • Explaining the reason or root cause behind a problem, which will give me even more motivation and drive to solving the obstacle, i.e., seeing the big-picture.
    What people misunderstand about me:
  • Being misunderstood as unapproachable, as I am often become silent and highly focused when problem-solving / completing a task. Meanwhile, I am more than happy to talk when needed!
